Why is reading is so enjoyable?

When we read, we escape into another world or another person and enter into an altered state of consciousness, which alleviates tension in the muscles and heart. The relaxing effect produced by books is not determined by the type or genre of book you are reading, as long as you can lose yourself in the narrative.
